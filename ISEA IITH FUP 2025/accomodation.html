Guest house accommodation can be availed on peyment for everyone attending the ISEA FUP on Wireless Security 2025.

However, breakfast and dinner will be your responsibility.

Hostel Accommodation

Hostel accommodation is can be availed on payment for the candidates attending the FUP on Wireless Security 2025.

  • Facilities Provided: Cot, Table, Chair, Mattress, Bedsheet, Blanket, and Pillow
We will provide lunch, refreshments, and training materials.
However, breakfast and dinner will be your responsibility and can be purchased from student dining halls near the hostel blocks.

Registration Fee Payment:

How to Register

  1. Fill out the Google Form to register.
  2. Make a payment of ₹350 (per day) and select the number of days you wish to stay.
  3. Upload the following in the Google Form:
    1. Payment receipt (via the provided QR code).
    2. A copy of your Student ID card.
Scan the QR code to make the payment

For any further assistance, please contact the hostel office at office.hostel@iith.ac.in with the subject line: ISEA FUP on Wireless Security 2025 and keeping ISEA team at copy.
